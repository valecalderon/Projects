## Silent Stalker 
Author: Valeria Calderon\
All right reserved

Silent stalker is a terror game made in Unix basically about a monster talk is stalking you and following the character when you are not watching it. You have to collect 3 potions in the arena in order to escape. 
All resources were open source free to use. Designs of intro page, instructions and credits are done in canva by me. 

When you open the SilentStalker application it takes you directly to the game no other instructions needed.
